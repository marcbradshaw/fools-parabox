Bio:

 

 

Zoë had the misfortune of being based on a space station picked by the Cybermen as the perfect place from which to launch another invasion of the Earth. Zoë found that life with the Doctor was more exciting than the bland existence of her own life and readily joined his in his travels. She remained with him until the Time Lords removed her and returned her to her previous life.

Additional Comments:

Zoë had a photographic memory and a mind that could give the Doctor’s a run for its money.
