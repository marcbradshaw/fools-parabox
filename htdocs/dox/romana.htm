Bio:

 

 

Romana was a Time Lady (female Time Lord) assigned by the White Guardian to help the Doctor locate the six segments of the Key to Time. Romana was a recent graduate of the Academy and scored higher than the Doctor himself. However she was not used to the world outside of Gallifrey’s Induction Barrier and came across at times as both haughty and naïve, At the successful conclusion to the quest she underwent regeneration….

Additional Comments:

Was working with the Doctor really so stressful it triggered a regeneration?

Bio:

 

 

Romana chose to regenerate into the appearance of Princess Astra, who was the living embodiment of the sixth segement of the Key to Time. It is thought this was an attempt to confuse the forces of the Black Guardian, or maybe she just liked the look. In this regeneration Romana was more confident and out going, though she did sometimes succumb to her lack of experience. Rather than return to Gallifrey, Romana stayed in E-Space with K-9 to aid the Tharils.

Additional Comments:

Romana firmly believes in the principle of ‘try before you buy’.
