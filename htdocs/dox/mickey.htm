Bio:

 

 

Mickey is Rose Tyler’s boyfriend and was drawn into the affairs of the Doctor when he was abducted by an Auton wheely bin so that he could be replaced by an Auton facsimile. More cowardly than Turlough on a bad day, Mickey was questioned five times by the police after Rose disappeared and was the victim of a whispering campaign instigated by her mother. These experiences have resulted in Mickey becoming a much more forceful and useful ally.

Additional Comments:

Mickey now owns a metal bin.
