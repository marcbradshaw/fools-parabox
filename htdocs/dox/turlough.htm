Bio:

 

 

Turlough was exiled from his own people for political reasons and ended up as a pupil in an English public school. Recruited by the Black Guardian to kill the Doctor he spent several episodes talking to a glowing crystal and failing to carry out his task. Eventually he won enlightenment and used it to break free from the Guardian’s influence. A fully-fledged coward, Turlough was able to return to his home planet after a political coup.

Additional Comments:

Mark Strickson was asked to die his hair ginger so viewers would not confuse the one in the school uniform with the one dressed for cricket……
