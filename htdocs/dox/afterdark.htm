After Dark

 

Darkness falls.

Descending without warning;

In shock life retreats.

Darkness remains.

The world sleeps,

As underground, plans form.

Darkness continues.

Memories of summer past,

May brighten hearts, but still

Darkness endures.

Hidden, a spark is found;

Nurtured like a seed, it grows.

Darkness falters.

Like a phoenix rising, light returns

A new dawn; the sun is reborn.

Darkness flees.

The land is glorious;

Bathed in sunlight.

Darkness no more.

A new future is upon us,

A bright potential for all.

Darkness will return,

But it will not last,

Even the longest night

Gives way to a new day.
