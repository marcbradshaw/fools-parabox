Bio:

 

 

Susan was the Doctor’s ‘grandaughter’ and as such is presumably of Gallifreyan if not Time Lord stock. She was bright and slightly telepathic but not beyond the normal teenage (if she was a teenager of course) bouts of petulance and angst. Susan was left behind by the Doctor when he noticed she was attracted to freedom fighter David Campbell who she presumably married (it was the early 60’s).

Additional Comments:

A noticeably older Susan was abducted by Borusa during The Five Doctors and showed that despite the years she could still trip over anything.
