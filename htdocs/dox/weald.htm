Goddess of the Weald

 

In the background She stands proud.

Silent.

Watching.

Waiting.

When time is right She will act.

Beautiful.

Deadly.

Glorious.

She teaches all who show their worth.

Truth.

Honour.

Dedication.

On battlefields of war and life.

Strong.

Enduring.

Victorious

Lifes cruel lessons She will teach.

Knowing.

Accepting.

Believing.

That which you find hard to see.

Opposites.

Composites.

Balance.

Mounted She rides the field.

March Maten.

Hare.

Crow.

Andred goddess of the Weald.
