Bio:

 

 

Sara Kingdom was an agent of the Space Security Service assigned to stop the Doctor in his terrorist activities by Mavic Chen, Guardian of the Solar System. When she realised that Chen was actually in league with the Daleks and had sold out humanity she quickly joined the Doctor in his efforts to foil the Daleks and their allies. Sara was killed at the end of this story when the Daleks’ Time Disruptor aged her to death.

Additional Comments:

With Katarina’s death earlier in the same story viewers must have been getting really worried when Sara also died.
