Bio:

 

 

Adric was an outler, an outcast from the community formed by the survivors of the star-liner that crashed on Alzarius in E-Space. However it was revealed that the real survivors were wiped out by the native Alzariun marsh people who then evolved into their human appearance. Adric was awarded a gold star for mathematical excellence and stowed away on the TARDIS after the death of his brother. Adric was killed trying to avert a Cyberman attack on Earth, the result of which was the extinction of the dinosaurs and therefore part of history.

Additional Comments:

Adric’s badge was edged in gold, something that the Cyberleader failed to appreciate.
