An Intro to the Fool

    or

A taste of what you are in for

 

Hail to you, and now its time,

To post my intro, its in rhyme.

I am a chap of forty odd,

I honour Herne, a randy sod.

A goddess too but not in mirth,

For Andred made me prove my worth.

It is an eclectic path I tread,

But though my ways were Wiccan lead,

I do not term myself a witch,

Simply Pagan, I use, so please don't bitch.

With mongrel blood I walk the Earth,

From Henry's court, where I saw my birth.

To London's suburb just down the way,

Where England's rugger is home to play.

With bladder, bells and pagan cool,

I greet you now, Kev the Cosmic Fool.
