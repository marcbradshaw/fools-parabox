Nature’s Voice

As seasons change and turn the wheel,

Nature’s rhythm you will feel.

From new buds green to branches bare,

Nature's voice is everywhere.

Be inspired and share your views,

Nature's wonder is your muse.

When sorrow struck and down you feel,

Nature's glory will help you heal.

As the path unwinds towards your goal,

Nature's light will guide your soul.

From Merry Meet to Merry Part,

Nature's song is in your heart.
